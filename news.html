STEM scholars visit PPMA Total Show

On the 3rd of October PPMA BEST hosted 89 students at the PPMA TOTAL Show 2019. The students were from schools from across the UK. The day was a great opportunity for them to find out about the innovative engineering that takes place and the career paths they have available to them.
